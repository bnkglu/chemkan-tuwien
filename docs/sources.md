### Kolmogorov–Arnold-Informed Neural Network (KAINN)

- **Related:** [Research on Solving PDEs of Solid Mechanics Based on PINN](https://github.com/yizheng-wang/Research-on-Solving-Partial-Differential-Equations-of-Solid-Mechanics-Based-on-PINN/tree/main)

- **Code:** [srigas/jaxKAN](https://github.com/srigas/jaxKAN)
